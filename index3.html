About Me

Dost Muhammad is a dedicated AI researcher with a BS (Hons) in Computer Science and an MS in Artificial Intelligence. Currently pursuing a PhD at the School of Computer Science, University of Galway, Ireland (CRT-AI & ADAPT centres).

His work focuses on Explainable AI (XAI), eXplainable Deep Learning, Responsible AI, Multimodal AI, Knowledge Graphs, and Medical Imaging.

Research Approach

My research follows an interdisciplinary approach, combining theoretical foundations with practical applications. I focus on developing AI systems that are not only accurate but also transparent, interpretable, and trustworthy.

By bridging the gap between complex AI models and human understanding, my work aims to make AI more accessible and reliable for critical applications in healthcare, finance, and other domains where decision transparency is essential.
