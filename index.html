About Me

Connect With Me

I am Dost Muhammad, an AI researcher specializing in Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI), deep learning, and medical image analysis. My work focuses on designing transparent and trustworthy AI models that support clinical decision-making, particularly in areas such as cancer detection and automated diagnostics.

My research integrates interpretable neural networks, responsible AI principles, and advanced deep learning architectures to create models that are not only accurate but also explainable and safe for real-world use. I aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and clinical reliability by making machine learning systems more understandable to healthcare professionals.

I have authored multiple publications in high-ranking Q1 journals, contributed to international conferences, and collaborated with researchers across domains. My work continues to support the global effort of building ethical, reliable, and human-centered artificial intelligence systems.

Research Focus

Exploring the frontiers of Explainable AI and its applications

Explainable AI (XAI)

Developing transparent and interpretable AI systems that provide insights into their decision-making processes for clinical applications.

eXplainable Deep Learning

Creating methods to interpret and explain complex deep learning models for various medical and financial applications.

Evaluation Metrics for XAI

Designing quantitative and qualitative metrics to assess the effectiveness and trustworthiness of XAI methods.

Multimodal AI Systems

Integrating multiple AI models and leveraging knowledge graphs for enhanced reasoning and explainability in complex systems.

Medical Imaging with AI

Applying AI and XAI techniques to medical imaging for improved cancer detection, diagnosis and treatment planning.

Trustworthy AI

Developing ethical, reliable, and human-centered AI systems that are safe for real-world clinical applications.
