About Me

Connect With Me

I am Dr. Mohammad Muazzam Sharif, an Assistant Professor of English with expertise in Pakistani literature, Shakespearean studies, and postcolonial literature. My research bridges traditional literary scholarship with contemporary critical approaches.

As a Fulbright alumnus and PhD graduate from the University of Southampton, UK, I bring international perspectives to my teaching and research. My work focuses on cross-cultural literary analysis, Shakespearean adaptations in South Asia, and the development of Pakistani literature in English.

With over 40 publications in national and international journals, I actively contribute to literary scholarship while mentoring the next generation of researchers and scholars at Abdul Wali Khan University Mardan.
