About Me

Connect With Me

I am Dr. Mohammad Muazzam Sharif, an accomplished assistant professor in the Department of English at Abdul Wali Khan University, Mardan, with over 16 years of experience in teaching Pakistani literature in English, modern fiction, contemporary poetry, research, and academic coordination across different public sector universities.

My work focuses on Shakespearean studies, Pakistani literature in English, postcolonial literature, and English language teaching. As a Fulbright alumnus of the FLTA program, I served as a language instructor at the University of Georgia, Athens, USA. I hold a PhD in English from the University of Southampton, UK, with expertise in drama and literary studies.

I have authored more than 40 research papers featured in national and international journals, with extensive experience presenting at global conferences and organizing seminars, workshops, and academic events. Fluent in English, Pashto, and Urdu, I demonstrate a strong commitment to advancing literary scholarship and fostering intercultural understanding through education.
